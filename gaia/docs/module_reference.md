# GAIA Module Reference

This document provides a breakdown of the main modules in the GAIA Superintelligence system.

---

## Core Modules

### intelligence_engine.py
Responsible for high-level cognitive tasks, meta-reasoning, decision trees, and advanced LLM logic orchestration.

### emotion_engine.py
Simulates emotional states, mood evolution, and integrates with both GUI and voice synthesis for real-time feedback.

### dream_state_engine.py
Manages nightly dream generation, strategic forecasting, emotional feedback loops, and reinforcement signals.

### self_repair.py
Performs integrity checks, diagnostics, and repairs to the AI core and plugin systems.

### plugin_registry.py
Registers, loads, and validates all third-party or internal plugin modules.

---

## Learning & Memory

### memory_manager.py
Handles short-term and long-term memory encoding, retrieval, and tagging of important sessions.

### neural_weights_updater.py
Manages self-optimization and fine-tuning of embedded models (LLMs, predictors, planners).

### self_reflection.py
Simulates daily self-analysis, updating goals, introspection logs, and emotional context tracking.

---

## Data & Training

### trainer.py
Controls supervised/reinforcement training sessions, supports synthetic dataset generation, model training cycles.

### sync_engine.py
Ensures federated learning support and secure peer-to-peer knowledge syncing across GAIA nodes.

---

## Export & Planning

### export_manager.py
Exports logs, strategies, predictions, and dreams into PDF/CSV/HTML formats.

### gcode_planner.py
Used for hardware design or robotic modules. Converts plans to G-code for 3D printing or CNC systems.

---

## GUI Modules

### dashboard.py
Main application interface containing navigation logic and intelligence/mood meters.

### settings_panel.py
Includes toggles for modes (Jarvis Mode, Max Power, Dream Sync), preferences, and UI configurations.

### mining_panel.py
Presents crypto mining stats, heat indicators, wallet sync, and performance toggles.

### voice_panel.py
