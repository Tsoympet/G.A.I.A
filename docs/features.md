📘 GAIA – Features Overview
GAIA (Gamblers Artificial Intelligent Assistant) is a modular, multi-domain AI platform engineered to assist users across online gambling, crypto, stock, and cognitive enhancement systems. It includes predictive logic, reinforcement learning, emotional modeling, and a Jarvis-like interactive interface.
